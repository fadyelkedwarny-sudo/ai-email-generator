AI Email Generator

Messenger WhatsApp Twitter Facebook Email

How to use

  • Type the subject of your email in the input box.
  • Click "Generate Email".
  • The AI will generate a full email based on your subject.
  • Copy the email and use it wherever you like.
  • Click "Share Email" to share your generated email on Messenger, WhatsApp, Twitter, Facebook, or via Email.
  • This works completely offline for generation, but sharing opens web links.
